1. What This Policy Covers

This policy applies to all internal AI bots developed by Carbon Vault Sdn Bhd, including Jarrod, Jarvis, Cyril, Sara, Maria, Aequitas, and Modvader. These bots are used internally by Carbon Vault employees and consultants for project support and operational efficiency.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect user input, project documentation, and system metadata. We do not collect sensitive personal data or data from individuals under 18 years of age.

3. Use of Artificial Intelligence Technologies

We use OpenAI GPT-4 and secured platforms like AWS and Pinecone. Data shared with these services is encrypted and governed by their respective data usage policies.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for internal operations, document generation, knowledge indexing, and project analysis. It is not shared for marketing or third-party purposes.

5. Data Sharing and Access

Access is limited to internal teams and secure system providers. We do not sell or share personal information externally.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained as long as needed for internal functions and is deleted or anonymized when no longer required.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Do Not Track

We currently do not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals.
